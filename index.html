Full Body · Strength & Muscle 20-Minute
Dumbbell Routine
Adjustable Dumbbells Only
3× Per Week
Beginner Friendly
Format 5 exercises · 3 rounds
Work 40 sec on
Rest 20 sec off
Start Weight 8 – 12 lbs
The Exercises
1
Goblet Squat Legs · Glutes

Hold one dumbbell vertically at your chest. Feet shoulder-width apart, squat down like sitting into a chair — chest up, knees tracking over toes.

▶ Watch: Goblet Squat for Beginners
2
Romanian Deadlift Hamstrings · Lower Back

Hold dumbbells in front of your thighs. Hinge at the hips — sending them back — while lowering the weights down your legs. Feel the stretch, then drive hips forward to stand.

▶ Watch: Dumbbell RDL for Beginners
3
Bent-Over Row Back · Biceps

Hinge forward at the hips, back flat. Pull the dumbbells up toward your hips, squeezing your shoulder blades together. Builds posture and pulling strength.

▶ Watch: Bent-Over Row Form
4
Shoulder Press Shoulders · Triceps

Seated or standing, dumbbells at shoulder height with palms forward. Press straight up until arms are extended, then lower slowly and with control.

▶ Watch: Dumbbell Shoulder Press
5
Floor Chest Press Chest · Triceps

Lie on the floor, dumbbells at chest height, elbows at ~45 degrees. Press up, lower slowly. The floor limits range of motion — joint-friendly and perfect for beginners.

▶ Watch: Floor Press for Beginners
Key Tips
— The slow lowering phase is where muscle is built. Don't rush it.
— Rest 60–90 seconds between rounds if needed, especially at first.
— After 2 weeks, if 3 rounds feels easy — add 2.5–5 lbs or a 4th round.
— Form first, weight second. Always.
